Snow Wampa’s Lair

Description

The snow wampa’s lair will be the starting location for the players.  The players will spawn finding themselves suspended from the roof of the back of the cave with limited gear strung about.  They must free themselves by any means necessary and either overcome or sneak past the snow wampa to escape.
